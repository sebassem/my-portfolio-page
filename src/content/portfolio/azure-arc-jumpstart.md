## Project Overview

Lead core maintainer for the Azure Arc Jumpstart project, contributing to its evolution over 4 years from a collection of samples to a fully-fledged engineering product used by enterprises and partners worldwide.

### Key Achievements

- **4-Year Evolution** - Helped transform the project into a comprehensive engineering product
- **Partner Adoption** - Project leveraged by major partners like SAP and Rubrik for product development and customer demos
- **Product Innovation** - Spearheaded the creation of Jumpstart Copilot, a new product initiative

### Reference Implementations

#### Contoso Hypermarket
Led the architecture and end-to-end automation of this comprehensive retail industry scenario featuring:
- AI-powered shopper analytics solutions
- Computer vision for customer insights
- Real-time monitoring dashboards
- Edge-to-cloud data pipelines
- Built on Kubernetes, powered by Azure services

#### Contoso Motors
Architected this manufacturing industry reference implementation including:
- Industrial IoT scenarios
- Edge computing patterns
- Cloud-to-edge connectivity
- Real-time telemetry and monitoring

### Jumpstart Copilot

- **Product Vision** - Conceptualized and led the product vision for this new AI-powered assistant
- **PRD Authorship** - Authored the comprehensive Product Requirement Document
- **Cross-functional Alignment** - Orchestrated alignment with engineering, legal, and design teams
- **FY26 Roadmap** - Secured inclusion in the FY26 product roadmap
